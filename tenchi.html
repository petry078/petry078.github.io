# Tenchi Security Newsletter #0

## Third Party Risk Management New Data, News & Opinion

War Games (1983)

New study reveals that organizations are not ready to handle the increasing third-party security incidents. The report, published by Prevalent, reckons that organizations aren't paying enough attention to non-IT security risks and disconnected tools inhibit third-party incident response and lengthen remediation. Also, recommends that companies should look at risks later in the third-party lifecycle.

The information security magazine, BankInfoSecurity, need you to participate on the "The State of Third-Party Security Risk Management Survey" to help the industry find the best tools, processes and challenges; understand the priorities for minimizing third-party security risks and identify drivers for success for better risk management. It only takes 5 minutes, they said.

The full report and the link to the to participate on the survey is available on section.
